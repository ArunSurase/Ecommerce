PG-DAC

The Post Graduate Diploma in Advanced Computing (PG-DAC) is the flagship programme of ACTS. The course is targeted towards engineers and IT professionals who wish to venture into the domain of advanced computing.
The course aims to groom the students to enable them to work on current technology scenarios as well as prepare them to keep pace with the changing face of technology and the requirements of the growing IT industry.
The course curriculum has been designed keeping in view the emerging trends in advanced computing as well as contemporary and futuristic human resource requirements of the ICT industry.
The entire course syllabus, course ware, teaching methodology and the course delivery have been derived from the rich research and development background of C-DAC.
The depth and width of the course is unique in the industry covering a wide spectrum of requirements of the ICT industry. Running successfully for more than seventeen years,
the PG-DAC course has yielded more than quarter million of students, who are well positioned in the industry today.
After the completion of the course, students get great career opportunities in the field of software development in good IT companies.

PG-DBDA

Post Graduate Diploma in Big Data Analytics (PG-DBDA) a 6-months full time PG-Diploma course of C-DAC ACTS.
This course has a unique focus of technologies related to big data like Hadoop, Cloud computing, Data visualization,
High performance computing as well as machine learning.
These are cutting edge, next generation advanced technologies that will shape the future of the aspiring candidates.
At present, candidates with these skills are in huge demand, especially in view of the current technological revolutions.
This course provides a comprehensive intense training for all the constituents of data analytics domain right from programming languages
and operating systems to HPC and machine learning thereby completing the entire technology spectrum.
After the completion of the course, students get great career opportunities in Software Development, Software analyst, data scientist etc.

PG-DASDM

Diploma in Advanced Software Development Methodologies (DASDM) is a training program designed specifically for Science graduates (BCA, BCS, BSC) and Engineers
who wish to make a career in the field of Software Development and Software Testing. The course imparts all necessary knowledge and develops skills in advanced technologies
related to software development and testing.
It lays a strong conceptual foundation of software development through modules like OOP using Java, Advanced Java and Database technologies. It also includes the most in-demand technologies
like 'Python' and 'Full-Stack Development' which definitely boosts the placement chances. Testing module convers concepts of testing as well as selenium and other mobile testing tools.
The course also provides 100 hrs of training on Aptitude Skills, Communication Skills and Soft-skills. All these skillsets will definitely make the students most suitable for recruitment
in the placement phase.
After the completion of the course, students get great career opportunities in the field of software development and testing in good IT companies.
